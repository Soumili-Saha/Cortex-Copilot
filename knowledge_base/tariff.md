# Electricity Tariff Information

Provider: TGSPDCL
Tariff category: HT CAT. 1A
Tariff name: Active tariff
Consumer type: Contract Demand (CD) 1501 kVA, 33 kV supply
Currency: INR (₹)

## Time-of-Day Energy Rates (per kVAh)

Off-Peak hours (22:00–06:00): ₹6.65/kVAh
Normal hours (10:00–18:00): ₹7.15/kVAh
Peak hours (06:00–10:00 and 18:00–22:00): ₹8.65/kVAh

## Demand and Fixed Charges

Contract Demand (CD): 1501 kVA
Minimum chargeable demand: 1201 kVA (80% of Contract Demand)
Demand charge — Normal: ₹500/kVA/month
Demand charge — Penal: ₹1000/kVA/month (for excess above Contract Demand)
Electricity duty: 6 paise/kVAh (6p flat)
Customer charges: ₹3,500/month
Late payment surcharge: 1.25% of bill (if paid after 15 days)
